Inside MayaTel


MayaTel comprises many divisions located throughout North America, with operations that extend throughout the world. The MayaTel Home Office, located in Slippery Rock, Pennsylvania, is home of MayaTel International, MayaTel North America, and other major corporate operations.
You can find out more about each of MayaTel's divisions below.

MayaTel Laboratories, Grove City, Pennsylvania
MayaTel Laboratories is the central research and development hub that provides the technological infrastructure driving our communications network.

MayaTel Pacific Command, Seattle, Wash.
MayaTel Pacific Command (MTPACCOM) provides essential business operations and infrastructure to our customers on the western seaboard.

MayaTel Central Customer Support Center, Death Valley, Nev.
The MayaTel Central Customer Support Center is the hub offering round-the-clock service and support for MayaTel's customers around the globe.

MayaTel International Telecommunications, Haneda Airport, Tokyo, Japan
MayaTel International Tellecommunications provides essential business operations and infrastructure to our customers abroad in the countries where we operate, which currently include Japan and Korea.

MayaTel Video, Slippery Rock, Pennsylvania
MayaTel Video is MayaTel's new state-of-the-art digital video rental/distribution service, available now to subscribers everywhere. Only available to households with broadband internet or faster.

MayaTel Home Internet, Inc., Washington, D.C. Coming soon!
MayaTel Home Internet provides Internet services, including high speed dial-up and dedicated access; web site hosting, development and maintenance; and other Internet-related services to businesses and consumers.
